About

The JavaScript Dojo or simply JS Dojo is a grand web app game where you train the art of the JavaScript to become a coding warrior and battle powerful foes such as Python, C#, Java, Ruby, C++, PHP, and find the 7 Bug Balls scattered around the world to become the Programmer King...

... or so what I originally planned to create it. In reality, you simply click the right buttons to progress the story and help the poor creator of this game to learn JavaScript who can't tell the difference between 'var' and 'let' variables!
